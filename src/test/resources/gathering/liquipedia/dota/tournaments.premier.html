From Liquipedia Dota 2 Wiki

Premier Tournaments offer a very large prize pool, are played out offline, and feature the best teams from around the world. They are commonly held by well-established franchises and are considered especially prestigious amongst the community.
Highlighted tournaments are held/sponsored by the publisher Valve and feature an outstanding prize pool. They are the biggest and most prestigious events on the tournament circuit.
